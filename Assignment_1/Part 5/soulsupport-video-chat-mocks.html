SoulSupport: Revised Video Chat Demo Mocks Plan

Based on the goal of creating a platform where users can video chat with a digital figure through speaking, we've revised our Demo Mocks Plan to focus on this immersive experience. This update will showcase SoulSupport's key feature of providing a real-time, face-to-face interaction with an AI-driven support figure.

Key Principles for Video Chat Demo Mocks:

  • Highlight the realism and responsiveness of the digital figure
  • Showcase the natural language processing capabilities for spoken interactions
  • Demonstrate the emotional intelligence and empathy of the AI through facial expressions and tone
  • Illustrate the seamless integration of video, audio, and optional text chat
  • Emphasize privacy and security features specific to video interactions

2. Emotion Recognition and Response

  • Visual cues showing the AI's recognition of user emotions
  • Demonstration of AI's facial expressions adapting to user's emotional state
  • Example of AI providing empathetic responses based on detected emotions

3. Session Recap and Progress Tracking

  • Post-session summary with key points discussed
  • Mood tracking based on emotion recognition during the session
  • Progress visualization comparing current session to previous ones

4. Personalization Options

  • AI figure customization (appearance, voice, personality traits)
  • Language and accent selection for diverse user base
  • Background environment options for the AI figure

5. Privacy and Security Controls

  • Options to blur background or use virtual background for user privacy
  • Clear indicators of encryption and data protection measures
  • Easy-to-access privacy mode toggle

6. Multimodal Interaction Demo

  • Seamless switching between video, audio-only, and text chat modes
  • Integration of screen sharing for showing resources or guided exercises
  • Optional AR features for interactive exercises or visualizations

Technical Considerations:

  • Ensure demo can showcase low-latency, high-quality video streaming
  • Implement mock voice recognition and natural language processing indicators
  • Demonstrate adaptive bitrate streaming for various network conditions
  • Highlight cross-platform compatibility (mobile, tablet, desktop)

Presentation Strategy:

  1. Create a simulated therapy session flow to demonstrate the video chat experience
  2. Prepare scripts showcasing the AI's ability to handle various user scenarios
  3. Highlight the technology behind the realistic AI figure (e.g., advanced animation, emotion recognition)
  4. Be ready to discuss privacy measures and ethical considerations of AI-driven video therapy

These revised HTML demo mocks will provide VCs with a compelling visualization of SoulSupport's innovative video chat feature. By focusing on the realism of the AI figure and the immersive nature of the interaction, we can effectively communicate the unique value proposition of our platform in the mental health support market.
